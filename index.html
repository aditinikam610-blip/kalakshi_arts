Our Story

Where Art Meets Tradition

Kalashi Arts is a passion project rooted in the rich visual culture of India. Founded by Devyani A. Pise, every piece we create is an act of love — painted by hand, crafted with intention, and made to carry stories.

From hand-painted jute bags celebrating classical dance motifs, to fabric-painted sarees featuring Mughal miniature art, to kurtis alive with mythology — we create wearable and giftable art that honours our heritage while feeling entirely contemporary.
